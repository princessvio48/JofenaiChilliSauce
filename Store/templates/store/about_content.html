About Jofenai Sauce

We are dealing with production of sauce using pure organic pepper as raw materials. We collect form the farm, grind and make a good package ready and suitable for using.

Our sauce contains habenaro, lemon and vineger that add food value, flavor and good taste. All these ingredients improves food for better health

Jofenai sauce provices flavor, moisture contrast and food color.
